富易堂诞生于2011年11月11日上午11时11分11秒——被称为「世纪光棍节」的历史时刻。这个百年一遇的日期，成为品牌基因中不可磨灭的印记。

创立之初，隶属于Cube集团旗下，与业界知名品牌188BET同属一家。Cube持有马恩岛及菲律宾PAGCOR双重牌照，为品牌运营奠定了合规基础。Prestige IOM更持有FCLRC互动博彩牌照至2024年，三地监管资质完整覆盖。

{{ else }}

FYT launched at 11:11:11 AM on November 11, 2011 — the once-in-a-century "Century Singles' Day." A timestamp you don't forget, and neither did the brand.

Born under the Cube group alongside 188BET, FYT started with Isle of Man and PAGCOR licenses from day one. Prestige IOM added an FCLRC interactive gaming license through 2024 — three jurisdictions of regulatory coverage before most competitors had one.

Cube集团是亚洲博彩行业深耕多年的集团公司，旗下最广为人知的品牌是188BET——一个在体育投注平台领域享有盛誉的名字。与188BET同属一家，意味着富易堂从第一天起就继承了成熟的运营经验与行业资源。

合规是信任的基石。马恩岛牌照在全球博彩监管中以严格和公信力著称；PAGCOR覆盖亚太市场的合规要求；FCLRC牌照提供菲律宾经济特区内的额外监管覆盖。三地资质在当时的行业中并不多见，为品牌注入了深厚的制度信用。

{{ else }}

Cube was a heavyweight in Asian gaming, and 188BET was its flagship — a name any serious sports bettor recognizes. Sharing that lineage gave FYT battle-tested operations and deep industry resources from the start.

Trust starts with compliance. Isle of Man — globally recognized for strict standards. PAGCOR — Asia-Pacific coverage. FCLRC — Philippine economic zone regulatory reach. Three jurisdictions before most competitors had sorted out one. That kind of foundation doesn't get built overnight.

在中文论坛的黄金时代，富易堂活跃于天朝论坛、玩博论坛、华人论坛、贝博论坛和海燕论坛，以口碑和真实互动建立信任。信任，是靠群体的口碑一点一点累积起来的。

品牌同样经历了蜕变：从富易堂 Riche88 到 FYTBET——传承不断，面貌常新。标语也从「要赢？一点都不难！」演变为「让博彩成为娱乐」，反映了品牌从竞技取向到娱乐优先的成熟转变。十余年来，变的是表达方式，不变的是服务承诺。

{{ else }}

When Chinese forums were the internet, FYT was there — Tianchao, Wanbo, Huaren, Betboy, Haiyan. Not buying banner ads, but joining conversations, responding to feedback, running community events. Trust earned the old-fashioned way.

The brand evolved from 富易堂 Riche88 to FYTBET. The tagline went from "Want to win? It's not hard at all!" to "Where betting becomes entertainment" — a shift from pure competition to a broader vision. Fifteen years on, the look has changed; the commitment to members hasn't.
