富易堂诞生于2011年11月11日上午11时11分11秒——被称为「世纪光棍节」的历史时刻。这个百年一遇的日期，成为品牌基因中不可磨灭的印记。

创立之初，隶属于Cube集团旗下，与业界知名品牌188BET同属一家。Cube持有马恩岛及菲律宾PAGCOR双重牌照，为品牌运营奠定了合规基础。PrestigeIM更持有FCLRC互动博彩牌照至2024年，三地监管资质完整覆盖。

{{ else }}

FYT was born at 11:11:11 AM on November 11, 2011 — a once-in-a-century moment known as "Century Singles' Day." This extraordinary timestamp became an indelible mark in the brand's DNA.

Founded under the Cube group alongside the renowned 188BET brand, FYT inherited licenses from the Isle of Man and PAGCOR. PrestigeIM held an FCLRC interactive gaming license through 2024 — establishing regulatory presence across three jurisdictions.
