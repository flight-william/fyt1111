The design team put Ah Fu in a #88 jersey — 88 being both the brand's code and an auspicious number. The red colorway was a nod to Manchester United, from the era when FYT first fell in love with football. Ah Fu in a jersey looks exactly like that one friend who's always the loudest at the stadium. {{ end }}

Three Men Make a Tiger, Cover the Sky with One Hand, A Drop in the Ocean… each idiom got a brand-new illustration with Ah Fu playing a different role. Over twenty paintings, no two alike. A betting brand doing cultural education sounds odd on paper, but Ah Fu wandering through ancient proverbs somehow just works. {{ end }}
