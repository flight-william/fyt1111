2015年，阿富来了。棕色卷发、红色背带裤、胸口一个「富」字——这个俏皮的卡通男孩，从此成了富易堂自己的品牌符号。

Ah Fu arrived in 2015 — brown swirl hair, red suspenders, the character "富" on his chest. Part mascot, part brand signature. If you know Ah Fu, you know FYT.

He's shown up at VIP events, charity matches, and in the social sticker sets that members use daily. Not a corporate mascot — more like an inside joke that only the community gets.

40多版反复打磨，每个表情、每个姿态都不将就。「没在怕」「Call Me」「BUSY」「BYE」「X TALK」——贴纸不用推广，在会员的朋友圈里自己就传开了。

周年纪念马克杯，一套十只，连环画风格讲阿富从破壳到成长的故事。每年春节还有生肖限定金像——金马、金羊、金猴，年年不同，只赠VIP。金箔扑克、名酒、表带、烟灰缸，件件限量，只送不卖。不是赠品，是平台记得你的方式。

{{ else }}

Over 40 design iterations went into getting every expression right. "No Fear," "Call Me," "BUSY," "BYE," "X TALK" — stickers that members actually use, spreading through VIP circles organically.

The standout collectible is the anniversary mug series — 10 pieces (0 through 9), each a comic-strip chapter of Ah Fu's story since 2015. Every Lunar New Year brings a zodiac golden Ah Fu figurine — Golden Horse, Golden Goat, Golden Monkey — a new edition annually, for VIPs only. Gold-plated playing cards, premium spirits, watch bands, ashtrays — none of it for sale. These are gifts reserved for the members who matter most.

The RICHE88 logo appears naturally in every panel, not as product placement, but as part of Ah Fu's world.
